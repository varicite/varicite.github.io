Varicite

Varicite is a producer of fobs. A Fob is an object that can take the form of a piece of jewelry or a counterweight. As jewelry, the qualities of the fob itself are enjoyed like a piece of art. Varicite makes fobs that are like jewelry or art. They have no real function other than to be enjoyed for their intrinsic qualities.

Fobs

The Current Series of Fobs

The current series of fobs consists of a series of square, circle and heart shapes. The fobs are laser cut from sheets of acrylic. These fobs come in different styles and sizes. The sizes are 30mm 60mm and 90mm wide. The styles are plain, pendant( with a hole for a string) and interlocking (slots cut into the fob so that they can interlock with other fobs).
